Creative Digital Imaging & Typography (30)

The ability to manipulate imagery has phenomenal impact on the way we work, our knowledge base, our society and our cultural horizons. A sound appreciation of typography and its application on its own and with imagery is an essential quality in any designer. This module seeks to introduce the technology behind digital imaging and typography in the form of industry-standard software and hardware; drawing on heritage and context and the wider effects on contemporary practice and society.
